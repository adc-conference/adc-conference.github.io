Call for Papers

Encore Track

To highlight recent impactful research and promote broader engagement within the community, ADC 2025 is pleased to continue the Encore Track. This track aims to showcase noteworthy work already published at top-tier venues, providing an opportunity for further visibility and fostering meaningful interactions among researchers.

We invite researchers to submit the title, abstract, and author details of their recent publications for an encore presentation at ADC. Submissions should be papers published, accepted, or in press since January 1, 2024, in well-regarded conferences (including but not limited to SIGMOD, VLDB, ICDE, EDBT, KDD and CIKM) or journals (including but not limited to IEEE TKDE, VLDB Journal, and ACM TODS).

Accepted submissions will be featured at the main conference via poster sessions or lightning talks.

Review Process

Submissions to the Encore track will not undergo a formal review process but will be subject to a basic check for relevance and quality. The goal of this track is to enrich the diversity of the conference program and stimulate engaging discussions.

Submission Guidelines

Authors are required to upload a PDF copy of the published or accepted paper, along with the official publisher URL. If the paper has been accepted but not yet officially published, please include evidence of acceptance (e.g., acceptance email) as a PDF. The process will start immediately after the submission.

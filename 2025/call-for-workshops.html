Call for Workshops

We invite proposals for workshops to be collocated with the ADC 2025 conference. The organisers of accepted workshops are required to announce the workshop and call for papers, solicit submissions, and conduct the reviewing process.

Submission Deadline: 15 August, 2025 (23:59 AEST UTC+10)

Notification Date: 31 August, 2025

Submission Guidelines

Workshop proposals should be submitted via email to Workshop Track Chair. Proposals must be in pdf and include the following (in the order given):

  • Workshop title.
  • Organisers.
  • Contact information.
  • A description of the workshop’s topic and its goals.
  • Desired workshop format, including preferred date and duration.
  • A description of the submission review process, including key dates and coverage of how conflicts of interest are handled.
  • Plans for publicity.
  • Potential (or accepted) program committee members.
  • Potential (or accepted) speakers or keynotes.
  • Historical information about each of the most recent previous editions of the workshop (if this is not its first edition).

Contact

For questions regarding the submission, please email the workshop chair:

  • Long Yuan (longyuan@whut.edu.cn, Wuhan University of Technology)

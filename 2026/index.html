    The 37th Australasian Database Conference

    Brisbane, Australia & Hengqin Island, China

    The University of Queensland & City University of Macau

    15 - 17 December, 2026

    The Australasian Database Conference (ADC) series is an annual forum for sharing the latest research progresses and novel applications of database systems, data management, data mining and data analytics for researchers and practitioners in these areas from Australia, New Zealand and in the world. The 37th edition of the Australasian Database Conference, ADC 2026, will be held in Brisbane, Australia and Hengqin, China. We welcome contributions related to all aspects of database theory and foundation, techniques, and applications.
